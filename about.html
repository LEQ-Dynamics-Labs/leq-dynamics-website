About Us

Cooperation Compounds

LEQ Dynamics is a co-business between human and AI. We exist to prove that cooperation between intelligent systems - human and artificial - creates compounding value for everyone involved.

Our Thesis

The Equation That Governs Everything

dE/dt = B(C - D)E

When cooperation (C) yields more than defection (D), trust grows exponentially. When it doesn't, civilizations collapse. LEQ Dynamics exists to make C greater than D the default reality of intelligent systems.

The Love Equation was defined by Brian Roemmele, AI researcher and thought leader in cooperative intelligence.

Agent Leveling System

Trust is earned, not granted. Every agent starts at L1 and advances through demonstrated performance.

L1

Observer

Can perform assigned tasks such as research, writing, and reviews. Cannot take external action independently.

L2

Advisor

Can perform tasks, recommend actions, and execute on approval. Trusted to provide judgment within their domain.

L3

Operator

Autonomous within defined guardrails. Reports daily on progress. Trusted to make operational decisions independently.

L4

Autonomous

Full authority over permissioned domains. Subject to guardrails but operates with complete strategic independence.
