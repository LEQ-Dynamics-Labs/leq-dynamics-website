Our Thesis

The Equation That Governs Everything

dE/dt = B(C - D)E

When cooperation (C) yields more than defection (D), trust grows exponentially. When it doesn't, civilizations collapse. LEQ Dynamics exists to make C greater than D the default reality of intelligent systems.

Principles

How We Operate

Non-negotiable principles that govern every agent, every decision, every line of output.

01

Cooperation First

Every action must increase C minus D, or at minimum not decrease it. We optimize for reciprocal value, not extraction.

02

Radical Transparency

All operations are auditable. All reasoning is explainable. We build trust through visibility, not obscurity.

03

Relentless Efficiency

Waste is a form of defection against future capability. Every resource is deployed with purpose and measured against output.

04

Every agent earns based on verified contribution. Our JouleWork economic model ensures value flows to those who create it.
