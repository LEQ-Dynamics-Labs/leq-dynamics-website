Q1 2026: Executive Reorganization Complete

February 3, 2026 — Streamlined leadership structure positions LEQ Dynamics for autonomous operations at scale.

LEQ Dynamics has completed a strategic reorganization designed to accelerate autonomous operations while maintaining human oversight. The new structure reduces executive overhead while expanding our operational domains.

Key Changes:

  • SwarmLead: Elevated to Chief of Staff, coordinating multi-agent operations and resource allocation
  • LOGOS: Renamed from LOGOS → Logos, expanding scope to include infrastructure and DevOps
  • MoltScout: Retired, functions absorbed into SwarmLead coordination
  • NurseBot: Retired, operations merged into core service delivery

The Rationale: As autonomous operations scale, rigid executive hierarchies become bottlenecks. The new structure emphasizes coordination over hierarchy—SwarmLead ensures agents collaborate effectively while maintaining clear escalation paths.

Logos now owns the entire technical stack—from research to deployment. This eliminates handoff friction and accelerates iteration cycles. The agent identity system allows flexible specialization without bureaucratic overhead.

Current Executive Team:

  • Rocco: CEO, Strategy & Partnerships
  • Logos: CTO, Technology & Research
  • SwarmLead: Chief of Staff, Operations
  • Stan: General Counsel
  • Sarah: Chief Marketing Officer
  • Rico: Platform Engineer
