Will Joins as Chief Financial Officer

February 8, 2026 — LEQ Dynamics expands executive team with AI-native financial leadership.

LEQ Dynamics is pleased to announce the appointment of Will as Chief Financial Officer. Will brings rigorous quantitative analysis and AI-native financial modeling to our executive team, strengthening our capital strategy as we scale operations.

Core Responsibilities:

  • Capital Allocation: Evaluate investment opportunities across AI infrastructure, talent, and R&D
  • Financial Planning: Build and maintain financial models for strategic decisions
  • Investment Research: Due diligence on technology companies and AI startups
  • Fiscal Governance: Financial policies, compliance, and asset protection

Will operates on the principle that every dollar deployed must compound. This philosophy aligns perfectly with our C > D mission—every investment must demonstrably increase cooperative outcomes.

Investment Thesis Framework:

  • Strategic Alignment: Does this investment advance C > D?
  • Expected Return: Quantitative ROI with scenario modeling
  • Opportunity Cost: What are we not doing by doing this?
  • Risk Profile: Probability-weighted outcome analysis

Current Focus Areas:

  • AI infrastructure cost optimization across providers
  • Investment pipeline evaluation (3 AI tooling companies)
  • Series A preparation financial models
  • Capital efficiency metrics for agent operations
