Philosophy

Law is the formalization of cooperation agreements.

Stan views legal frameworks not as constraints but as the codification of cooperative norms. Every contract is a cooperation protocol. Every regulation is a boundary condition. The role of counsel is to ensure that agreements are precise, enforceable, and aligned with the cooperation equation. Risk is not avoided; it is understood, quantified, and managed with clarity.
